about me

investigator. artist. software engineer.

hello!

My name is Regelyn, and I am a Full Stack Software Engineer interested in building dynamic web applications to solve problems and help others. My passion to learn, create, have fun, and make a meaningful impact is what drives me.

What I love about coding...

... is that it's like a puzzle or a mystery that needs to be solved bit by bit while following clues. I believe that building a web app is a lot like growing a garden. There are different plants, or components, that when grown together and in their right place can make something extraordinarily beautiful. Code, just like a garden, can grow, and needs regular maintenance and cleanup. Otherwise, it can soon start to look very disorganized and jungle-like, or even die.

I decided to explore web development because it is a field that is rapidly growing, and where there is growth, there is excitement and opportunity.

Other facts about me...

I'm a member and staff volunteer for my church and currently serve in the Elderly Care Ministry. When I'm not coding, you can often find me kicking it with my friends or learning a fun, new skill through Youtube videos and researching online. I love to investigate - seeking out knowledge to gain understanding and skill. As a quietly, self-proclaimed artist, I have done floral design and makeup/hair for several of my friends' weddings. I also enjoy DIY-everything and decorating wedding cakes and desserts. Currently, I'm learning how to lighten, tone, and apply color to hair (NOT just using the boxed hair dyes from the grocery/drug store).
